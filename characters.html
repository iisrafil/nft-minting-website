Brody (MZ-1 Cyborg)

The gentleman. It's not everyday you see a robot dressed in suits to and hates dirt. The assistant behind the creation of the artificial bodies and the new metaverse. Calm headed but goal oriented. An Egomaniac with a smart mind which has the thirst of creating a new world order along with his master, doctor xeltra.

Draven (MZ-1 Cyborg)

He seems to possess a trait of sympathy. Enjoys long walks around the savaged lands and always says "I'm sorry", after bringing down a target. Being a master strategist he’s always one step ahead of the rest. The calmest of all the cyborg’s but berserk when troubled.

Revon (MZ-1 Cyborg)

His level of uninterest is alarming. No one ever knows his motives, not even doctor xeltra. But he shows how loyal he can be. Usually he enjoys solitude and lurking in the shadows carrying two shadow blades behind him, you wouldn't believe he has hands till it's time for its use

Gabriel (MZ-1 Cyborg)

The prodigy. Doctor Xeltra saw him as his best creation, but he never unlocked his true potential. Innocent and always eager to learn about the world he passes time watching animes and movies of the past world. He carries a vision never seen before.

Brent (MZ-1 Cyborg)

Razz, arrogant and believes he is the perfect creation. You wouldn't want getting in his way on any given day. He enjoys reading books about early humanity but doesn't sway from his missions, the only one of the creations which seems to a vibe for punk music.

Azrail (MZ-1 Cyborg)

The devil wears a machine. He really doesn't care who is in the area of operation. Be it an ally or a target if you move expect an attack. One of the reasons he isn't taken on missions that much. Always kept in a cage because he is way too dangerous to be a free bird.

Brenna (MZ-1 Cyborg)

A close friend of gabriel and an adventurous soul with an intent of blood, she hates humans due to her past life experience. She surges into battle with careless abandon, knowing his skin is the toughest and is hard to kill. Try taking away his gun and watch her go berserk.

Alastor (MZ-1 Cyborg)

The supposed big brother to all creations. He doesn't act until he is called upon. Although his appearance is a bit scary, he looks handsome, on robot standards and has quick reflexes always appearing to be the only creation who went to the gym without going to the gym.

Laila(MZ-1 Cyborg)

The sleek seductress. Part time stripper, part time killer Her red and dark designs is just a show of how sinister yet compelling she can be. Obedient and always, carrying out her masters will. She does everything in her power to be recognized above other creations.

Vlasta (MZ-1 Cyborg)

She simply loves blowing stuffs. A master of explosives. Spends most of her time watching historical wars on television just to see things go boom. Always keeps an evil smile while at her job. Not without question one of the most sarcastic and annoying cyborg of all.
