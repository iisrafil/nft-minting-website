About The Project

TheDark4ce is a collection of characters referenced from a future comic book.The selection presently focuses on 10250 hand drawn sketches of rogue cyborg characters which contain more than 300 features and gears that make them unique and lethal.

The Story

Hundreds of years into the future, the depraved minds of the lands have accumulated enormous wealth thanks to the Invention of Vitalik Buterin’s, Ethereum. With this newfound wealth they funded future technological innovations, leading to the ground-breaking discoveries, thus they came up with a system where if one has sufficient crypto, transcending mortality and bypassing the laws of space and time would be a possibility.

Harnessing this great power, the elites in society began to fractionalize their souls, storing them in decentralized databases creating nearly indestructible beings. Humanity has been able to bypass the restrictions of nature hence they start living in metaverse’s. Without an Organic body, age is but a number, cells never either, living becomes timeless,

But nothing is never enough, the daily clashes and, an act of survival of the fitness graces the land... Bring feud amongst the people and resulting in chaos in the ethereal realm. Hate, vengeance, and greed becomes the order of the day. The creator of this new innovations blames himself for this shortcoming. He, by chance creates genetically engineered superbeing’s with robotic abilities, the ultimate cyborgs which he uses as part of his plan to commit mass genocide and bring the land into a one world government where suffering and crimes do not exist. His motives are right, but two evils can't make a good.

Denizens of the ethereal metaverse are unknown to the coming danger, would there be an unlikely hero that would stop him and bring him back to the light? Or would he achieve his goal? We take you on the journey to reveal the outcome, as we also, do not know.
