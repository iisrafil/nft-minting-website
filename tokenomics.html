The Origin Drop: Minting of Membership Passes and MZ-1 Cyborgs NFTs
Membership Passes and the very first collection, MZ-1 Cyborgs, will arrive together, and the distribution will follow a very specific schedule that includes five stages:
  • a) Whitelisted Membership Pass Minting. Community members whitelisted for purchase of membership passes will be able to submit a transaction and mint a membership pass. Whitelisting for membership passes expires once the period has ended. Everyone who desires to use their whitelisted pass minting opp absolutely MUST do so before the period ends or forfeit the opp.
  • b) Public Membership Pass Minting. Any caller is able to purchase a single membership pass. The number of membership passes in each tier available at this stage will depend on the number of membership passes already minted during the previous stage, up to the totals indicated in the Medium article (link above).
  • c) Membership Collection Minting. Holders of membership passes can now mint MZ-1 Cyborg NFTs at their convenience, at gas cost. Each membership pass has a tier limit that defines how many Cyborgs that particular pass is allowed to mint. This period is not time-limited, but we strongly advise membership pass holders to mint prior to collection reveal and definitely prior to the date of Uploading when the whole collection, including tokens yet to be minted, will become immutable, but, more importantly, open to viewing. This could result in gas war skirmishes between membership pass holders, so you might want to avoid that.
  • d) Whitelisted Public Sale. Community members whitelisted for public sale will be able to mint one MZ-1 Cyborg each. Up to 500 addresses will be whitelisted prior to this period and potentially during the period. All the whitelisted addresses absolutely MUST purchase their Cyborgs before the period ends or forfeit the opp.
  • e) Public Sale. A minimum of 2,500 Cyborg NFTs will be available in this period for everyone to purchase on first come, first served basis as seen on the Ethereum network.
